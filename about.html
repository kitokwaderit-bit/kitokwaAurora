    • Accountability & Reporting

    Aurora publishes audited annual reports, program evaluations, and a project-level open dataset that allows donors and researchers to track outputs, outcomes, and expenditures. Our governance includes an independent board of trustees and a community advisory panel drawn from program regions.

    Financial Overview (FY2024)

    Total revenue: $42.8M — Program spending: $34.9M — Admin & fundraising: $7.9M.

    We commit to at least 80% of donor funds directed to program service delivery, with clear earmarking for restricted grants.
