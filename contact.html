Donate — Make a Direct Impact

We accept donations via credit card, bank transfer, and major global partners. Donors can choose an unrestricted gift or support a specific program. Donor reports and project updates are available to all contributors.

Donation Options

  • One-time donation — Immediate impact for the highest-need programs.
  • Monthly support — Sustained funding to scale programs responsibly.
  • Sponsor a child or school — Directly support school-level improvements.

Volunteer & Internships

Aurora recruits international and local volunteers across many disciplines. Volunteers are embedded in local teams and receive orientation, safety briefings, and mentorship. Internships are available year-round and include a small stipend in many locations.
